Delivery SLA Dashboard Insights

Problem Statement

The objective of this analysis is to identify key delivery routes that are causing SLA breaches and propose solutions for improving delivery timeliness.

  • Inefficient Deliveries: SLA breaches across specific routes affecting timely deliveries.
  • Underperformance on Certain Routes: Certain routes have a higher breach rate, indicating inefficiencies or delays.
  • Delivery Timeliness: A significant portion of deliveries are failing to meet SLA, resulting in customer dissatisfaction.

Key Data Insights

  • Volume Breakdown: Same Day deliveries make up the majority of orders, with 746 deliveries out of 1084.
  • SLA Achievement: Route 1 and 3 have an achievement rate of 83%, while Route 2, 4, 5, and 6 have a better performance at 94% SLA achievement.
  • Breaches Analysis: Route 1 and 3 have a significant SLA breach count (218 breaches), indicating areas for improvement.
  • Time-based Analysis: A high volume of Same Day deliveries needs optimization in the delivery process for improved SLA achievement.

Actionable Insights and Solutions

1. Route 1 and 3 Improvement:

  • Investigate delays or inefficiencies on these routes. Optimize routing and increase fleet capacity for better performance.
  • Consider using real-time tracking to identify bottlenecks during delivery hours.

2. Focus on Same-Day Deliveries:

  • Increase operational efficiency to handle the largest volume of Same Day deliveries.
  • Optimize last-mile delivery and consider additional resources during peak times.

3. Reduce Minor SLA Breaches in Route 2, 4, 5, and 6:

  • Monitor and address the small percentage (6%) of breaches to enhance overall performance.
  • Focus on timing patterns and improve traffic management for smoother deliveries.

4. Analyze Delivery Volumes:

  • Given the high volume of Same Day deliveries, explore strategies for peak time delivery optimization.
  • Scale operations during busy periods to meet customer expectations.
